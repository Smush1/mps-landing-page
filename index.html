FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Why Should I Choose “MPS” As My Dream Destination?

Education is a journey that instills the best of human values, imparting competence, capabilities, and a vision to empower and face the challenges of the world. Here at Dr. MPS Group of Institutions, our aim is not only to produce the best professionals for society but also sound entrepreneurs who can contribute to making this country a developed one.

How Can I Clear My Doubts In Case Of Any Query?

For any queries regarding the admission procedure, you can contact the student advisor either in person on the college premises or telephonically at 9359090501 or 9359090502. Additionally, you can send your queries via email at admissions@mpsgroup.org.in, available round the clock.

What Kind Of Placement Support Does The College Provide?

We have strong industry connections to arrange suitable and timely placements in both multinational and Indian companies. To facilitate this, Dr. MPS Group of Institutions has established a “Corporate Resource Centre”, staffed with a team of professionals dedicated to our students' futures. In addition to a focused study pattern, students will receive comprehensive placement training, including various tests and preparation through mock group discussions and interviews to ensure they are well-prepared in every aspect.

What Are The Eligibility Criteria For Applying To Dr. MPS Group of Institutions?

  • Bachelor's Degree Courses: Candidates must have completed 10+2 with a minimum of 50% marks from any recognized board or university.

  • Master's Degree Courses: Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks or an equivalent CGPA from any recognized university.

  • Biotechnology and Hotel Management: Admission requires passing a Common Entrance Test (CET). The CET dates can be confirmed from the college office.

  • Industry Integrated MBA:A separate entrance test is conducted. Shortlisted candidates based on test performance will be called for a group discussion followed by a personal interview.

  • BBA & BCA Courses: Admission is based on group discussion and interview. The names of the selected candidates will be announced on the Institute's website around the same time.

What Is The Mode Of Payment Of Fees? Is There Any Provision For Scholarships/Concessions?

Fees can be paid by cheque (local), demand draft, or cash. Dr. MPS Group of Institutions is committed to ensuring that no student is denied the opportunity to pursue their studies due to financial constraints. Substantial need-based scholarships are available for students from economically disadvantaged families. The institute also assists students in obtaining educational loans from financial institutions, subject to meeting the required conditions. Additionally, the institute awards merit scholarships and offers a 10% tuition fee waiver to students belonging to SC/ST categories.
